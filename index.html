Patch Test Information

Lash extension adhesive holds the potential of an allergic reaction. A patch test is carried out by applying a drop of glue behind the ear. This test cannot guarantee that an allergic reaction will not occur. Lash allergies can develop over time, and reactions may still occur at any time, even if the patch test shows no reaction.
